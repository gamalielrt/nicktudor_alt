I’m a carpenter based between London and Essex, designing and making bespoke furniture, buildings and architectural features. I favour clean, functional forms that promote the materials I use, and to highlight the ornamentation I employ sparingly.

It’s important to me that my work reflects the character of its surroundings, and I value the privilege to work within context of a persons life. I want to create sustainable outcomes of lasting quality; to contribute positive change to the built environment.
